About Our Project

We are students who want to make our world a better place. Our website is about Atarskaya Meadow. We would like people to help protect nature, take care of the meadow and become volunteers.

Our Team

Our team is friendly and creative! We work together to write texts, find interesting facts, take photos and build this site. Everybody has their own role. We want to make a difference!

Goals and Plans

We want to inform people about nature in our region and show why it is important to protect plants, animals and clean the environment. Our plan is to share new ideas and invite everyone to join us. Let's save nature together!
